Privacy Policy

App: SumSum | Developer: oandh | Package: com.oandh.sumsum

Contact: owandhs@gmail.com

Effective date: 2025-09-29

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service. Ads are provided by Google AdMob, and authentication/leaderboards by Unity Gaming Services (UGS).

1. Information We Process

  • No direct identifiers collected by us (no accounts, emails, phone numbers).
  • Third-party SDK processing
    • Google AdMob (ads): Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA), approximate location (e.g., IP-based), device info (model/OS), ad impressions/clicks.
    • UGS (auth/leaderboards): anonymous Unity Player ID, nickname (user-provided or auto-generated), scores, and IP/technical data processed for operations.
  • On-device storage: certain preferences/scores may be stored locally on your device.

2. Purposes

Provide game features (leaderboards), prevent abuse, serve/measure ads, and improve stability/quality.

3. Sharing & International Transfers

  • Google LLC (AdMob) – ads & measurement; processed globally (incl. US).
  • Unity Technologies (UGS) – auth/leaderboards; processed globally (incl. US).

    We do not sell personal information and do not share it beyond the third-party processing described in this policy. Third-party retention/processing follow their policies.

4. Retention & Deletion

We do not host personal data on our own servers. UGS/AdMob retain data per their policies. We will relay access/deletion requests to them upon contact.

5. Your Rights & How to Exercise

You may request access, correction, or deletion. We may ask for your Player ID to verify your identity. Contact: owandhs@gmail.com.

6. Your Choices

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of ads personalization (reset Advertising ID)
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" (ATT)

7. Inactivity & Deletion

If you do not use the app or submit scores for 12 months, we may delete or anonymize data linked to your anonymous Unity Player ID (nickname/scores). Deletion may occur without prior notice, except where notice is required by law. Third-party deletions follow their policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect children's data; if we learn of such collection, we will delete it. Ad delivery complies with AdMob policies, with child-related restrictions applied where appropriate.

9. Security

Minimal collection, encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and vendor contractual safeguards.

10. Changes

We will post updates in-app or on the store page.
