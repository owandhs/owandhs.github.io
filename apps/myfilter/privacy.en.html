Privacy Policy

App Name: My Filter | Developer: oandh | Package: com.oandh.myfilter

Contact: owandhs@gmail.com

Effective Date: October 24, 2025

My Filter collects and processes only the minimal data necessary to provide camera, photo, and video-editing features. We do not collect personal information for advertising, analytics, or tracking purposes.

1. Information We Collect

Type Data How It's Collected Notes
Required None – No account registration or login required.
Optional None – No email, phone number, or other personally identifiable data collected.
Device Permissions Camera, Microphone, Photos/Media access Used only with user consent For shooting and saving content.
Local Storage Captured photos/videos, settings, filter data Stored only on the user's device Never uploaded to a server.

2. Purpose of Use

  • To enable photo and video capture, editing, and saving.
  • To improve app stability and performance (in a non-identifiable manner).
  • To analyze app crashes and errors for maintenance.

3. Third-Party Sharing & Data Transfer

  • This app does not include any third-party SDKs for ads, analytics, or social logins.
  • No data is transferred to the developer's servers or external cloud services.
  • If you manually export files to iCloud, Google Drive, or other platforms, their own privacy policies apply.

4. Retention & Deletion

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Deleting the app or its data removes all associated content permanently.

5. User Rights

  • You may delete all data at any time by removing the app.
  • For questions or support, contact owandhs@gmail.com.

6. Payment & Refunds

  • This app is sold as a one-time purchase through App Store / Google Play.
  • All payments, refunds, and subscription management follow each store's policies:
    • Apple: https://support.apple.com/billing
    • Google: https://support.google.com/googleplay

7. Children's Privacy

  • This app is not directed at children.
  • If we become aware that we have collected children's information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Security Measures

  • Minimal data collection principle
  • Explicit consent for app permissions
  • HTTPS encryption for all transmission

9. Policy Changes

  • We will notify users of any changes to this policy within the app or on the store page.
